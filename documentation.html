What is Bugscout

BugScout is a pocket friendly tool that helps farmers identify disease symptoms early in the growing season. According to the FAO (Food and Agricultural Organisation), 20%-40% of global crop production is lost to pests annually. Poor crop yields are caused by the inability of farmers to effectively keep a check on plant health to ensure timely disease prevention.


Knowledege Gap

Currently, farmers have to use magnifying glasses when looking at disease symptoms. Also, they have to carry along a healthy plant for comparison purposes. These methods are archaic, prone to error and are tedious.


Solution

BugScout will provide a user-friendly interface where farmers can visually compare and differentiate healthy from diseased plants. Consequently, farmers can make timely decisions regarding fungicide application before the disease infestation and economic losses occur. Also, the tool will prevent disease diagnosis errors


Utilities
  • Plants: Provides a list of plant names present in the database
  • Healthy Plants: Depicts a graphical image of a healthy plant selected on the Plants menu
  • Diseased Plants: Shows the image of a diseased plant followed by descriptions of the disease, as well as possible treatment regimen
  • Diseases: Provides a list of other diseases associated with the selected plant. Clicking on any disease will provide a graphical image of the diseased plant.
